Master Mobile UX

- We should design fo the smallest, focus on the content and core functionality. That is the key to make a simple and beautiful app.
- The paradigm shift. People use more phones than computers. Moble traffic is up.
- Build for mobile first."Just because your website is responsive, it doesn't mean it's mobile first."
- Responsive Retrofitting: that's taking what you already have, and making it work on mobile. It is cheap, and prove a better experience.
- Mobile is not a fad.
- People use their THUMS to reach the phone. Reachability matters.
- Placement matters.
- Understanding matters.
- "The Hamburger" is not very useful.
- Reduce large information.
- Before adverstasing, research.
- No pop-up.
